5) Focus state: remove ugly outline (but stay accessible)

When you press Tab to navigate, browsers show a default outline (often a blue ring). It can look inconsistent with your design.

‚ö†Ô∏è Accessibility note: Do not remove focus styles without replacing them. Keyboard users must see where focus is.

5.1 Replace outline with a Tailwind ring
